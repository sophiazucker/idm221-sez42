About

​My name is Sophia Zucker, and I am a 21-year-old Music Photographer based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. I started photographing concerts when I was fifteen years old. Since then I’ve gotten the chance to photograph hundreds of shows under multiple digital publications, all while being a full-time student. I’ve had the opportunity to shoot shows of all sizes, from basements to amphitheaters to festivals. Music Photography is so important to me, and I plan on shooting shows as long as I possibly can. When I’m not photographing concerts, I’m either eating pasta or training to become a professional Mario Kart Wii player.
